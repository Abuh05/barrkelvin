LITIGATION

Handling of representation before the Supreme Court, The Court of Appeal, Federal High Court, the State High Courts, National Industrial Courts and Investments and Securities Tribunals in disputes without limitations to; - Administrative Law, Torts, Contracts and other Commercial Transactions, Corporate Matters, Banking and Insurance, Land Law, Criminal Law, Taxation, Intellectual Property, Property and Conveyance, Landlord and Tenancy, Personal Injury and Damage to Property, Recovery of Debts (within and outside Nigeria) and all forms of Guarantees.

COMMERCIAL LAW

Advising, preparation and perfection of securities and financial documents , Legal Services relating to state and federal taxes in planning and implementing decisions having a tax effect on benefit; Advising on effect and implication of various documentation involved in International Trade, Negotiation and Drafting of International Commercial contracts, Hire Purchase transactions. Tax advisory services, Mortgage transactions.

FOREIGN INVESTMENT

Advising on laws and procedures relating to foreign investment in Nigeria; Establishment of Companies with foreign interest; Obtaining requisite approvals (Expatriate Quota positions; Business Permits, Securities and Exchange Control Approvals; Immigration requirements etc.) for Companies and foreign participation in doing business in Nigeria; General legal support for new foreign investors.

OIL AND GAS

Advising on compliance with Nigerian Petroleum Laws and Regulations; Advising and Procuring various permits, Licenses and Approvals required for participation in Oil and Gas industry; Effecting Licenses and accreditation with the Nigerian Upstream Petroleum Regulatory Commission (NUPRC) and Nigerian Midstream and Downstream Petroleum Regulatory Authority (NMDPRA), Oil Service Companies, Traders, Financiers, etc.; Obtaining consent for assignment of various leases operating in the industry in line with the PIA; Review and preparation of oil industry type agreements (e.g. Petroleum Joint Ventures, Joint Operating Agreement, Risk service contracts etc.); Advising and representation in Disputes occasioned by damages resulting from production and distribution activities with respect to host communities of onshore and offshore facilities.

CORPORATE PRACTICE

Incorporation of Limited Liability Companies and Incorporated Trustees in Nigeria; Advising and Effecting compliance with mandatory statutory returns to the Corporate Affairs Commission and other relevant bodies; Advising and perfecting instructions with respect to Re-Organizations of companies, Mergers and Acquisitions, Take-Over of companies; Company Secretarial Services; Registration of Partnerships and Sole Proprietorships, Advising and Effecting contentious and non-contentious liquidations, Receiverships, Diversification effects, Schemes of Arrangement and Reconstruction; Asset purchases, Sales, Leases, Exchanges and other contracts related to operating business, public issues, restructuring, joint ventures; Advising on compliance with corporate registration especially banking, insurance; Business laws and Stock Exchange, and Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) requirements; Preparation of Contracts of Employment, Staff Trust Deed, Pension Funds, and Obtaining regulatory approval thereof.

REAL PROPERTY

Negotiation of Leases, Conveyances and other interests in landed property; Preparation of legal documents pertaining to various interests in property; Obtaining Certificates of Occupancy and consents to transaction in land and general perfection of title to property throughout Nigeria; Execution of searches at the Land Registries and advising thereon; Enforcement of terms of leases within the provisions of the law; advising and preparation of building leases and agreements and contractor finance contracts.

ENTERTAINMENT LAW

Advising on Contractual agreements and its legal consequences, Drafting and negotiating development and production contracts, such as writer agreements, talent agreements, and recording agreements., Facilitating and negotiating distribution deals for an entertainment project, Working on financing agreements for sponsorship, bank loans, co-production investments, grants, and other types of investments, Reviewing and commenting on pitches and budgets for television series, films, mobile apps or other entertainment projects, Resolving contract disputes through alternative dispute resolution (Mediation, conciliation or arbitration), litigation for copyright infringement, trademark infringement, breach of contract and other related matters

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Advising on laws and procedures relating to registration of trademarks, patents and industrial designs; effecting compliance with laws on administration of copyright in Nigeria. Advising and representation in disputes regarding infringement of intellectual property rights.
