He was born into the family of HON. JUSTICE ENYINANYA JAMES EJELONU (RTD) in Abia State, Nigeria. Attended Library Avenue Primary School, Umuahia, Abia State and Federal Government College Jos where he obtained his West African School Certificate in 1991. Then proceeded to University Of Abuja in 1992 where he graduated with a Bachelor Of Laws (LL.B) in 1997. He attended the Nigerian Law School and was called to The Nigerian Bar in September, 1999. He was posted to Bayelsa State for his Mandatory National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) In December 1999, on conclusion of the programme of which he served with the Bayelsa State Judiciary. He joined the Law Firm of B.S. IKAH & CO in Yenagoa Bayelsa State in 2001 and later on in 2003 joined the Law Firm of ABILA, SYL & ASSOCIATES still in Yenagoa, Bayelsa State, of which he gained invaluable experience in the practice of law particularly as regards litigation and corporate law given the diverse nature of cases handled by the above mentioned Law Firms. With the valuable experience gained, he then opened his own Law Firm KELVIN EJELONU & ASSOCIATES Yenagoa Bayelsa State in 2006

Kelvin Ejelonu is widely travelled and has quite some significant contacts in the legal profession in Nigeria especially as he was a National Officer of the Nigerian Bar Association(NBA) for four(4) years and several other positions in NBA National of which some of them are: National Welfare Secretary(2012-2014), National Financial Secretary (2014-2016), Member NBA 2014, Tech-Committee on Conference Planning, Member NBA Taskforce on Niger Delta 2016-2018 Secretary, NBA National Appeal Panel for Branch Elections 2020, Member NBA Law Reform Committee (2020-2022) Member, National Executive Committee (NEC) Of NBA from 2012 till date. He is an ardent and consistent participant in NBA Conferences, Mandatory and Continuing Legal Education (MCLE) programs and has series of certificates to that effect.

He has also attended several seminars and conferences on business law, corporate law, oil and gas matters and apart from being a Member of NBA, he is also a Member of NBA-Section of Public Interest Development Law (SPIDEL), Eastern Bar Forum (EBF), African Bar Association (ABA) Kelvin Ejelonu is also a Notary Public, his main practice areas are litigation, corporate, oil and gas of which he has handled some cases in the above mentioned areas of law right up to Court of Appeal and Supreme Court.
